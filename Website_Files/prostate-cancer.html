About



This Prostate Cancer App was created with the intention of gathering useful information
from a prostate cancer patient. The user, that is the patient will be able to create an account, with this
account, they will be able to enter their data. Also, they will be able to upload their data as well as
evaluat such data. They will also be able to send their data to their primary care physician or doctor
at some point in time when the app is fully functional,
to be looked at and asses.
